About

Michael Sharman is a senior web application developer for Learnosity who has been building and architecting web solutions for over 13 years in a variety of organisations ranging from digital marketing agencies to educational consultancies.

He has gained strong experience in both being a part of and leading agile development teams, and has also provided training local and abroad for the FarCry application framework.

Michael is also a firm believer in web standards and object oriented software design, dislikes music on flash sites where you can’t find the controls to turn it off and also struggles with the signal to noise ratio on twitter :(

  • PHP
  • JavaScript
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • SQL (Relational Databases such as MSSQL and MySQL)

A London Gift

Carl Orr is in town but only for a few days. Carl is truly a special guitarist and an old teacher of mine. In fact I even did his original wesite waaaay back in the day (please forgive it now!). Carl has been living in London for about 10 years so it was a long time coming considering I used to see him play almost every other week in the mid 90’s.

The gig was at the Seymour Centre and was put on my the good fellows at SIMA. It was the first time I’ve been there and it’s a really nice ‘club’, I liked it and will be back!

Also I ran into a friend of mine from UNI days which was a nice surprise.
